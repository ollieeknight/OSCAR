## Software and Tools

### Cell Ranger
10x Genomics. (2024). [Cell Ranger Software Suite](https://www.10xgenomics.com/support/software/cell-ranger/downloads).

### Cell Ranger ATAC
10x Genomics. (2024). [Cell Ranger ATAC Software Suite](https://support.10xgenomics.com/single-cell-atac/software/downloads/latest).

### FastQC
Andrews, S. (2010). [FastQC: A Quality Control Tool for High Throughput Sequence Data](https://www.bioinformatics.babraham.ac.uk/projects/fastqc/).  
[https://github.com/s-andrews/FastQC](https://github.com/s-andrews/FastQC)

### MultiQC
Ewels, Philip, et al. (2016). [MultiQC: summarize analysis results for multiple tools and samples in a single report](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btw354). *Bioinformatics*, 32(19), 3047-3048.  
[https://github.com/MultiQC/MultiQC](https://github.com/MultiQC/MultiQC)

### CellBender
Fleming, Stephen J., et al. (2023). [Unsupervised removal of systematic background noise from droplet-based single-cell experiments using CellBender](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41592-023-01634-8). *Nature Methods*, 20(9), 1323-1335.  
[https://github.com/broadinstitute/CellBender](https://github.com/broadinstitute/CellBender)

### cellsnp-lite
Huang, Xianjie, and Yuanhua Huang. (2021). [Cellsnp-lite: an efficient tool for genotyping single cells](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btab178). *Bioinformatics*, 37(23), 4569-4571.  
[https://github.com/single-cell-genetics/cellsnp-lite](https://github.com/single-cell-genetics/cellsnp-lite)

### Vireo
Sinha, R., et al. (2020). [Vireo: a scalable approach to analyze single-cell RNA-seq data](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btaa668). *Bioinformatics*, 36(14), 4247-4249.  
[https://github.com/single-cell-genetics/vireo](https://github.com/single-cell-genetics/vireo)

### AMULET
Thibodeau, Asa, et al. (2021). [AMULET: a novel read count-based method for effective multiplet detection from single nucleus ATAC-seq data](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-021-01336-w). *Genome Biology*, 22(1), 1-19.  
[https://github.com/UcarLab/AMULET](https://github.com/UcarLab/AMULET)

### mgatk
Lareau, Caleb A., et al. (2021). [Massively parallel single-cell mitochondrial DNA genotyping and chromatin profiling](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41587-021-00844-4). *Nature Biotechnology*, 39(4), 451-461.  
[https://github.com/caleblareau/mgatk](https://github.com/caleblareau/mgatk)

### KITE
Gehring, Jase, et al. (2020). [Highly multiplexed single-cell RNA-seq by DNA oligonucleotide tagging of cellular proteins](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41587-019-0392-3). *Nature Biotechnology*, 38(1), 35-38.  
[https://github.com/pachterlab/kite](https://github.com/pachterlab/kite)
